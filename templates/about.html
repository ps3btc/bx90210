about tweetandbeat.com

home

in early 2010, i decided to visit twitter.com to see if anyone out there is tweeting their experiences with the disease cancer. to my surprise i was amazed at how people share such personal information. i also found hoards. someone needed to curate these results. and so i decided to build tweet and beat a "vertical" search on top of twitter only for disease cancer. the following were my primary reasons for building the service:

first: wouldn't it be great to give a few words of encouragement when someone tweets something such as this?

My family has been fighting cancer for 3 years now. Have lost so far 2 out of 5 with it. I am also a widow from heart disease. (tweet)

or tell them (a perfect stranger) that you are happy for them, when they have a victory such as this?

Longest 10 days of my life. Cancer-free is all that matters today! Bad mammogram...breathing HUGEST sigh of relief. Can now get brain back! (tweet)

each one of us has so much empathy, so much love to give others so let us give this love to ones who are suffering (or know someone who is suffering) from the dreaded disease in real time. they tweet, why not offer a few encouraging words?

second: was to get people with the disease, to discover others with the disease in real time.

third: was to remove spam and build a social network on top of these cancer related tweets. you can favorite these tweets, and in general curate and personalize the experience just for yourself.

i sincerly hope you find this service useful. you can contact me anytime using the link below. if you feel passionate about the disease and wish to improve the look and feel of this service, i'd love to hear from you.
